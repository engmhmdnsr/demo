Tanks Maintenance and Cleaning

SAC Arabia provides comprehensive Tanks Maintenance and Cleaning services, ensuring the highest standards of quality, safety, and efficiency for industrial plants across KSA and the Gulf region.

OUR Tanks Maintenance and Cleaning SERVICES INCLUDE:
  • Product Transfer, Emptying & Cleaning
  • Re-positioning Roof Support Legs
  • Installation of New Cathodic protection system for tank including Design
  • Repair of Internal Floating Roofs
  • Sludge Removal & Disposal
  • Decontamination & Degassing
  • Inspection, Tank Scanning & Hydro Testing
  • Installation/Replacement of Float Roof Seals
  • Repair/Replacement of Bottom Plate, Annular Plate, Roof/Float Roofs
  • Blasting & Painting/Coating & Lining
  • Cold Cutting, Decommissioning and Demolition of Tanks
